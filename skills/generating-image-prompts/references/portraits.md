# Portraits

Vocabulary for portrait photography prompts.

## Cropping / Framing

| Term | Frame |
|------|-------|
| Extreme close-up | Eyes or partial face only |
| Close-up | Face fills the frame |
| Head shot | Head and neck |
| Head and shoulders | Head to mid-chest |
| Bust shot | Head to just below chest |
| Half body / Waist-up | Head to waist |
| Three-quarter | Head to mid-thigh |
| Full body | Entire figure, head to toe |

## Poses

- Looking at camera, looking away, profile view
- Over-the-shoulder, tilted head
- Arms crossed, hands in pockets, leaning
- Walking, sitting, standing

## Expression Keywords

- Confident, pensive, serene, intense, playful
- Authoritative, self-assured, fierce, mysterious
- Warm, approachable, candid, stoic

## Lighting Styles

| Style | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| Rembrandt | Triangle of light on cheek, dramatic |
| Butterfly | Shadow under nose, glamorous |
| Split | Half face lit, half in shadow |
| Rim/edge | Backlit outline, separation from background |
| Broad | Lit side facing camera, flattering |
| Short | Shadow side facing camera, slimming |
| Natural/soft | Even, diffused, outdoor feel |
| High-key | Bright, minimal shadows |
| Low-key | Dark, dramatic shadows |

## Photography Styles
