# Interior Design Vocabulary

Vocabulary for interior design and room visualization prompts.

## Interior Styles

| Style | Characteristics |
|-------|-----------------|
| Scandinavian | light wood, white walls, functional, cozy |
| Minimalist | essential furniture, clean lines, uncluttered |
| Contemporary | current trends, neutral palette, mixed textures |
| Mid-century modern | organic shapes, wood, iconic furniture |
| Industrial | exposed brick, metal, open ceilings |
| Japandi | Japanese + Scandinavian, zen, natural |
| Rustic | natural materials, warm, textured |
| Bohemian | eclectic, layered textiles, plants |
| Modern farmhouse | white, wood, comfortable, practical |
| Art deco | geometric, luxe materials, bold colors |
